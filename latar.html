Company's Background

SELGATE SPECIALIST HOSPITAL RAWANG is one of the health facilities under the management of SELGATE Healthcare, a subsidiary of the Development Agency of Selangor (PKNS). This hospital was established with the aim to provide high quality health services and to belong to the community. The escalating cost of healthcare has prevented many patients from receiving the quality care they need. Selgate Specialist Hospitals is dedicated to narrowing this gap by ensuring healthcare remains accessible to all. Alongside a commitment to efficiency and productivity, the Group's primary goal is to deliver outstanding healthcare services that are within reach for every individual and community.

Located strategically in Rawang, the hospital is equipped with the latest medical equipment and advanced technology. It operates with full commitment to ensure that every patient receives the best treatment.

To be the leading health service provider and trusted, providing high quality care centered on patients for the welfare of the community.

To create value in the healthcare sector, benefiting the state's economy, its people, and providing balanced returns to our stakeholders.

  • 01 To provide holistic, quality, and patient-centered healthcare services.
  • 02 To become a center of medical excellence equipped with the latest technology and qualified medical specialists.
  • 03 To ensure access to affordable healthcare for all segments of society.
  • 04 To enhance community health awareness through education and disease prevention programs.
